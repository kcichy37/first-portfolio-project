About us

At Letchworth Sports Centre we are about promoting physical activity and participation in sports to better people around us. the Sports Centre caters to a wide demographic from all ages and abilities, we want to ensure that everyone who is participating and using our facilities are in a safe environment and that they leave satisfied by our grounds and happier & healthier after using our services.
